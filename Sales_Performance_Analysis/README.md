Sales Performance Analysis
Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from a small retail business to identify top-performing products, city performance, sales trends, and key business insights.

Objectives
Clean the dataset.
Calculate business KPIs.
Analyze product and city performance.
Detect missing values and outliers.
Build charts and dashboards.
Make business recommendations.
Tools Used
Python (Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib)
Excel
Power BI
Dataset

The dataset contains order information including:

Order ID
Product
City
Units Sold
Price
Total Revenue
KPIs
Total Revenue
Average Order Value
Total Units Sold
Number of Orders
Revenue by Product
Revenue by City
Key Findings
Chocolate generated the highest total revenue.
Los Angeles had the highest revenue among all cities.
Units sold and revenue showed a strong positive correlation.
One record (Milkshake) contained missing values and was excluded from calculations where necessary.
Recommendation

Continue promoting high-performing products like Chocolate while testing promotions on lower-performing products or cities. Improve data quality by ensuring missing values are addressed before analysis.
